Frequently Asked Questions

What kinds of equipment can you install?

While we can install and maintain any tools, our focus is on creating an environment friendly to hobbyists. We aim to primarily install the tools your employees might want or have at home, but at a quality level that may not be practical for them to own.

Why should we choose you?

With years of experience building and maintaining shared workshops, we know what is important in a hobby shop, what breaks, and how to find and maintain the best equipment.

What happens if something breaks?

We will fix anything we install, offering regular maintenance plans or individual repairs based on your needs.

How can my team learn to use the tools?

We strive to design spaces to teach skills through unsupervised exploration and experimentation, however we are happy to provide guided instruction on anything we install.

Why do I need a hobby workshop in my business?
  • A workshop provides a great place for your workers to exercise their creativity and problem solving. A place to experiment and explore gives your team unique chances to hone their innovative skills.
  • It is a place for informal collaboration, not only helping your team to work better together, but giving opportunities to share friendly feedback between people that might not get chances to communicate with each other often.
  • More employees are seeking hybrid and remote jobs that avoid the office, with our workshops you can convert the disused space into a haven for collaboration and creativity.
