Our Process

  • Discover
  • Design

Initial Discovery

We work with you to find the right options for your shop. Through observation of the space, we find what opportunities can be unlocked within it.

Equipment Selection

Selections of equipment based on your needs, be it a 3d printer and a sewing machine or a full shop where you could build just about anything. Designs are presented for your approval.

When we have a plan you're happy with, we build out the space, installing any fixtures and commissioning any equipment so that it's ready to use.

Once the shop is built, we can provide maintenance and training on any equipment in it scaling to the needs of your business.
